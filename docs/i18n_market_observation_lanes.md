# MindReply I18N And Market Observation Lanes

This is an internal owner/reporting note. It is not public website copy.

## Active Private Lanes

1. Language Fidelity Lane
   - Verify the homepage, footer, contact route, package page, pricing path, and MRagent shell keep readable language in all supported locales.
   - Supported public selector languages: English, Spanish, French, German, Portuguese, Arabic, Hindi, Japanese, Chinese, Ukrainian.
   - Arabic must keep right-to-left document direction. Other languages stay left-to-right.
   - Do not claim full professional translation unless the whole route and generated output have been reviewed.

2. Country Signal Lane
   - Treat IP country as the first hint, browser language as the second hint, and manual selector as the user override.
   - Keep UK and US first for immediate buying intent.
   - Keep UAE and Saudi Arabia high for premium private-client and executive-service demand.
   - Keep Germany, France, Japan, Brazil, Spain, and India in the first ten for business communication demand and language coverage.

3. Mobile Presentation Lane
   - Check for horizontal overflow, oversized hero gaps, selector wrapping, footer wrapping, and CTA crowding on phone screens.
   - The language selector must stay visible but quiet; it should not compete with MRagent or the package CTA.
   - Footer contact must route through the contact form and MRagent, never a personal email.

4. Search Positioning Lane
   - Prioritize buyer-intent language around website completion, response overload, follow-up pressure, professional lexicon calibration, and privacy-safe assisted close.
   - Use priority markets in metadata and sitemap language alternates.
   - Do not publish internal staff counts, agent-control language, worktree notes, tokens, or owner strategy.

5. Evidence Lane
   - Every hourly report must separate three states: committed to GitHub, deployed to Vercel, visible on the custom domain.
   - If the custom domain is stale, the report must say stale instead of implying success.
   - Live proof should include homepage, geo locale, robots, sitemap, and health where available.

## Current Revenue Rule

Every language, SEO, and mobile change must increase at least one of:
- speed to first relief,
- trust,
- payment or invoice clarity,
- MRagent usage,
- Website Completion Package confidence.

Anything else is paused until the public surface is stable and the domain reflects the latest GitHub commit.
