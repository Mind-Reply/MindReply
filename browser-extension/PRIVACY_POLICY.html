MindReply Browser Extension - Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 18, 2026

Overview

MindReply is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how the MindReply browser extension collects, uses, and protects your data.

What Data We Collect

✓ Collected & Sent to Our Server

  • Text you paste into the extension (only when you click "Get Move")
  • Your email (optional, for receiving results)
  • Browser type and version (for compatibility)

✗ NOT Collected

  • Your browsing history
  • Cookies or tracking pixels
  • Personal information (unless you provide it)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers

How We Use Your Data

  1. To analyze your text and provide recommendations
  2. To improve our AI analysis models
  3. To send you analysis results (if email provided)
  4. To comply with legal obligations

Data Storage

  • Locally: Analysis history stored in browser storage only (never uploaded)
  • On Server: Text you analyze stored for 30 days, then auto-deleted
  • Backup: Not backed up or shared with third parties

Data Security

  • All data transmitted via HTTPS (encrypted in transit)
  • Servers use industry-standard encryption
  • No third-party ad networks or trackers
  • No cookies or fingerprinting

Third-Party Services

We do NOT share data with:

  • Google Analytics or other analytics services
  • Advertising networks
  • Social media platforms
  • Other third parties

Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Delete: Ask us to delete your analysis history
  • Export: Get your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Uninstall the extension anytime

Extension Permissions

The extension requests these permissions:

  • activeTab: To see the current tab URL
  • scripting: To detect selected text on pages
  • storage: To save your analysis history locally
  • contextMenus: To add "Analyze with MindReply" option

Children's Privacy

MindReply is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted at https://mind-reply.com/privacy
